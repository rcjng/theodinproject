Hot Dog

Description

The above image is the final result of this recipe: a freshly assembled and garnished hot dog.

Grab the below ingredients and follow the below steps to make one yourself!

Ingredients:

  • Hot dog wieners
  • Hot dog/sausage buns
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps:

  1. Grill hot dogs for ~5 minutes
  2. Open buns and place hot dog in bun
  3. Drizzle mustard over the hot dog
  4. Drizzle ketchup over the hot dog
