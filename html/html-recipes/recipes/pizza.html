Pizza

Description

The above image is the final result of this recipe: a freshly oven-baked Margherita pizza.

Grab the below ingredients and follow the below steps to make one yourself!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. Prepare pizza dough
  2. Preheat oven adn pizza steel/stone
  3. Assemble the pizza
  4. Bake pizza for 7-8 minutes
  5. Slice pizza into 8-10 slices
