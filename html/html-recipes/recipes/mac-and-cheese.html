Mac & Cheese

Description

The above image is the final result of this recipe: a freshly baked pan of mac and cheese.

Grab the below ingredients and follow the below steps to make one yourself!

Ingredients:

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Eggs
  • Heavy cream
  • Shredded cheddar
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Shredded gouda
  • Cajun seasoning

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 385 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cook macaroni until 3 minutes less than fully cooked before rinsing under cold water until cooled
  3. Mix eggs, heavy cream, and cajun seasoning in a medium bowl
  4. Pour the egg, heavy cream, and cajun seasoning mixture over the cooked pasta and stir to combine
  5. Toss together the cheddar, mozzarella, and gouda in a large bowl before adding to the pasta
  6. Transfer the mixture to a 9-by-13-inch baking dish
  7. Bake until the center of the mac and cheese is set and the top is golden brown or 60-70 minutes, then cool for ~10 minutes
